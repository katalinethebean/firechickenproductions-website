Our Story

Fire Chicken is a student-led business that sells merchandise featuring teachers (with consent!). We wanted to raise funds for Senegal, but knew we could do something more creative than a standard bake sale.

The idea started while sitting in physics class, realizing we wanted a tangible, creative way to remember our teacher. We wanted to build something lasting that creates real impact abroad while bringing people together on campus.

By creating a shared campus initiative and a common purpose outside of traditional grading and lectures, we are breaking down classroom barriers and building a tighter, more connected school community.
