What we support

Causes we donate to.

Cause #1

Heep Hong Society

Heep Hong Society is a non-profit closing the education equity gap, supporting youths with diverse needs. Every 10 shirts supports 1 child with developmental disorder, providing integrated training in physical strength, social, self-care, intelligence and speech.

Cause #2

Tai Po Fire Relief

Following a devastating fire in the Tai Po district, this local relief initiative provided immediate aid and essential supplies to displaced families.

Cause #3

Les Cajoutiers

A school in Warang, Senegal, providing free education and resources to underprivileged children, including support for deaf and hard-of-hearing students.

Current campaign

Summer 2026 fundraising goal.

Every month, we aim to donate HKD 500 to Heep Hong Society. Staying consistent with monthly HKD 500 donations helps 6 children with developmental disorder to have integrated training in physical strength, social, self-care, intelligence and speech every year. Exceeded amounts will either be donated to Heep Hong Society, other causes, or saved for future manufacturing.
