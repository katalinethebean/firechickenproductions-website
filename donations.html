100% of our profits go directly to causes we believe in. Here's who we support and how we're doing.

What we support

Causes we donate to.

Cause 1

Heep Hong Society

Heep Hong Society is a non-profit closing the education equity gap, supporting youths with diverse needs. Every 10 shirts supports 1 child with developmental disorder, providing integrated training in physical strength, social, self-care, intelligence and speech.

Learn more →
Cause 2

Tai Po Fire Relief

Following a devastating fire in the Tai Po district, this local relief initiative provided immediate aid and essential supplies to displaced families. We quickly directed all revenues to Save The Children to help those affected.

Learn more →
Cause 3

Les Cajoutiers

A school in Warang, Senegal, providing free education and resources to underprivileged children, including support for deaf and hard-of-hearing students. Our team traveled to Senegal to meet the kids firsthand.

Current campaign

Summer 2026 fundraising goal.

Every month, we aim to donate HKD 500 to Heep Hong Society. Staying consistent with monthly HKD 500 donations helps 6 children with developmental disorder have integrated training every year. Exceeded amounts will be donated to Heep Hong Society, other causes, or saved for future manufacturing.
